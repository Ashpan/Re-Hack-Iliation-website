Re-Hack-Ilation

65% of Americans that come out of surviving a neck or arm injury, face many muscle pains in their arms; they find simply moving their arm up and down a chore. This piece of software utilizes the Leap Motion to encourage users to move their arms very slowly and gives them motivation. The software includes a small game to allow users to move around different objects and create different shapes by using their muscle movements which in turn will allow the user to heal their muscles.
